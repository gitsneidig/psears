Residencies and Workshops

Currently Oregon's seventh poet laureate, Peter Sears has taught at Reed College and in the MFA program at Pacific University, served as Dean of Students at Bard College, and as community services director for the Oregon Arts Commission. He actively facilitates writing workshops through Portland-based Mountain Writers, in addition to independent workshops in Portland and Corvallis, Oregon.

University and program teaching

  • Community of Writers, 1998 - 2002. Taught workshops for teachers, and evaluated writers in follow-up residencies
  • Iowa State University, instructor, composition, 1989 - 1990
  • Bard College, instructor, humanities, 1982 - 1983
  • Reed College, writer-in-residence, 1974 - 1976, 1977 - 1980
  • Lewis & Clark College, adjunct faculty, poetry writing, 1974 - 1976, 1978 - 1980
  • International College, Beirut, Lebanon, instructor in English, 1963 - 1964

High school and middle school full-time English teaching

  • Sidwell Friends School, Albuquerque Academy, Princeton Day School, and Bedford Rippowarm School, grades 7 - 12, 1965 - 1984.
