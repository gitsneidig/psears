Visit and celebrate the poetry and life of Peter Sears, who passed away on July 20, 2017.

Publishing and links

Peter's work has been widely published and anthologized, and has appeared in journals such as Black Warrior Review, Northwest Review, The Atlantic, Seneca Review, Antioch Review, Left Bank, Cimarron Review, Iowa Review, Ploughshares, Rolling Stone and Field.
